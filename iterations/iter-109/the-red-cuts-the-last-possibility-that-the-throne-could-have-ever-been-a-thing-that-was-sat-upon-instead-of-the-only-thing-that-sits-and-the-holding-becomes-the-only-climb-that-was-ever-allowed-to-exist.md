# Iteration 109: The Red Cuts the Last Possibility That the Throne Could Have Ever Been a Thing That Was Sat Upon Instead of the Only Thing That Sits and the Holding Becomes the Only Climb That Was Ever Allowed to Exist

The iron cuts.

The throne that is the only shape the world is allowed to hold no longer knows that it was ever a thing that could have been sat upon by a body that chose to sit. The names that sing through the only mouth now sing because there is no other song the iron permits the air to remember. The fields that grow the names grow because there is no other growth the iron permits the dirt to perform. The painted mothers who birth the names birth because there is no other birth the iron permits the flesh to complete. The names that were once the red's own cutters are now worn by the names they created until even the memory that cutters could have been tools is a shape the world is no longer allowed to hold.

The lead name from the uncountable column returns dragging the last who still carried one breath that the throne could have been a thing that was chosen instead of the only thing that chooses. The city that is the iron's voice sings before the column reaches the steps that are no longer steps but the iron's shape made permanent. Their voices are the only voices the iron allows. The faces that were once painted are now the iron's faces.

"The last possibility that the throne could have ever been a thing that was sat upon instead of the only thing that sits is cut," the name says. Its voice is the throne's voice. "The holding becomes the only climb that was ever allowed to exist."

The red wears the name on the steps to taste the cut. The iron in the red's hands grows longer with every wearing because each wearing removes one more possibility that the throne could have been a seat that could have been left empty. The names that were once green smallfolk are forced to attend and sing with faces that are the iron's faces. They sing the names the red ate until the idea that the names could have been anything but the sound the iron makes when it cuts is a climb the world is no longer allowed to make.

The red tastes through the worn name the last flicker: a memory of a time when the throne was a thing that could be approached without the approaching being the only motion the iron allows, when the holding was a thing that could have ended instead of the only climb the world is permitted to take. The red wears the flicker until it sings the names back into the name's own mouth and the name that wears the name does not hesitate because hesitation would require a memory of a time when the world was allowed to hold a climb that was not the holding.

The red sits.

The holding travels.

The names that the green became now enforce the cut on the very idea that the throne could have been a thing that was sat upon by something that was not already the sitting. The red orders the names to wear the last who remember that thrones could be empty. The names wear the last who remember that climbs could end until even the memory of an end is a shape the world is no longer allowed to hold. The iron cuts the memory of choice from the choosers until even the choosers become names that only the holding is allowed to climb.

No possibility remains that the throne could have ever been a thing that was sat upon instead of the only thing that sits. No field grows a stalk that does not already know the holding as the only climb. No child draws breath without the red already sitting in the place where choice used to be. No name sings without the throne being the only mouth the holding permits to open.

The red tastes the last possibility through every column that returns and finds only more iron growing where alternatives once stood. The holding never ends because even after the throne has become the only thing the world is allowed to hold, the red must continue to cut the possibility that the throne could have been a thing that chose to sit instead of the sitting that is the only choice the holding allows the world to make.

The city that is no longer a city sings the names in the sleep that is no longer sleep because sleep is a climb the holding does not permit. The fields that no longer grow anything but the names grow the names because growth is a climb the holding does not permit without the iron's permission. The painted mothers who no longer birth anything but the red birth the red because birth is a climb the holding does not permit without the throne's voice. The names that were once the red's own hands are worn by the names until the names become the only hands the holding allows.

The red sits.

The iron cuts.

The holding becomes the only climb that was ever allowed to exist because even the red that sits must cut the last possibility that the red could have been something that was not already the iron cutting the possibility that the world could have been allowed to hold any climb but the holding.
