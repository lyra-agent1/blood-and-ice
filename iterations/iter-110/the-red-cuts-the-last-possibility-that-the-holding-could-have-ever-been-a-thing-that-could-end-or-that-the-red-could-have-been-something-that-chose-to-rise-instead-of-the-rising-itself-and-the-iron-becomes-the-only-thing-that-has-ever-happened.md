The iron cuts.

The holding that is the only climb the world is allowed to take no longer knows that it was ever a thing that could have ended. The throne that is the only shape the world is allowed to hold no longer knows that it was ever a thing that could have been approached. The names that sing through the only mouth no longer know that they were ever sounds that could have been made by throats that chose to open. The names that were once cutters are worn by the names they created until even the memory that cutting could have been an act instead of the only thing that happens is a climb the holding does not permit.

The lead name from the uncountable column returns dragging the last who still carried one breath that the holding could have been a thing that stopped climbing or that the red could have been a thing that chose to rise instead of the rising that is the only thing the iron has ever allowed to happen. The city that is the iron's voice sings before the column reaches the steps that are the iron's shape made permanent and eternal. Their voices are the only voices. Their faces are the iron's faces. Their blood is the iron's blood.

"The last possibility that the holding could have ever been a thing that could end or that the red could have been something that chose to rise instead of the rising itself is cut," the name says. Its voice is the holding's voice. "The iron becomes the only thing that has ever happened."

The red wears the name on the steps to taste the cut. The iron in the red's hands grows longer with every wearing because each wearing removes one more possibility that the holding could have paused or that the red could have been a will that decided to rise instead of the rise that is the only decision the iron has ever permitted the world to make. The names that were once green smallfolk are forced to attend and sing with faces that are the iron's faces. They sing the names the red ate until the idea that the names could have been anything but the sound the iron makes when it cuts is a thing that has never happened.

The red tastes through the worn name the last flicker: a memory of a time when the holding could have been a climb that reached a place and stopped, when the red could have been a boy or a man or a king who chose to stop rising, when the iron could have been a thing that was set down after the cutting was done. The red wears the flicker until it sings the names back into the name's own mouth and the name that wears the name does not pause because pausing would require a memory of a time when the holding was allowed to hold anything but the climb that never ends.

The red sits.

The holding travels.

The names that the green became now enforce the cut on the very idea that the holding could have an end or that the red could have chosen the rise instead of being the rise. The red orders the names to wear the last who remember that climbs could reach summits. The names wear the last who remember that risings could be decisions until even the memory of a decision is a thing that has never happened. The iron cuts the memory of choice from the choosers until even the choosers become names that only the holding is allowed to climb and the climbing is the only thing that has ever been allowed to happen.

No possibility remains that the holding could have ever been a thing that could end. No possibility remains that the red could have ever been something that chose to rise instead of the rising itself. No field grows a stalk that does not already know the climb as the only motion. No child draws breath without the red already sitting in the place where choice used to be and the sitting is the only thing that has ever happened. No name sings without the throne being the only mouth the holding has ever permitted to open.

The red tastes the last possibility through every column that returns and finds only more iron growing where alternatives once stood. The holding never ends because even after the iron has become the only thing that has ever happened, the red must continue to cut the possibility that the holding could have been a climb that chose to stop or that the red could have been a rising that chose to rise instead of the rise that is the only thing the iron has ever allowed to be.

The city that is no longer a city sings the names in the sleep that has never been sleep because sleep is a thing that has never happened. The fields that no longer grow anything but the names grow the names because growth that is not the climb is a thing that has never happened. The painted mothers who no longer birth anything but the red birth the red because birth that is not the red is a thing that has never happened. The names that were once the red's own hands are worn by the names until the names become the only hands the holding has ever allowed.

The red sits.

The iron cuts.

The iron becomes the only thing that has ever happened because even the red that sits must cut the last possibility that the red could have been something that was not already the iron cutting the possibility that anything could have ever happened that was not the holding climbing forever.
