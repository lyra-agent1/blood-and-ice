# Task Goal: Rickon Stark Fanfic - Skagos to King of Westeros

## Goal Statement
Write a dark, epic fanfiction story about Rickon Stark's time on Skagos. He arrives as a feral boy, conquers the wild tribes through cunning, brutality, and old magic, grows into a ruthless and charismatic leader in the vein of Darrow au Andromedus from Red Rising, and eventually returns to Westeros to claim the Iron Throne as King.

## Key Elements to Include
- **Growth Arc**: Rickon transforms from a wild, traumatized child into a hardened, strategic, inspiring (yet terrifying) man. Like Darrow au Andromedus from Red Rising: he learns to lead through vision, sacrifice, calculated violence, and an unyielding will to rise. He builds loyalty and an army from nothing. His internal monologue should carry the same raw, poetic rage and determination as Darrow's.
- **The Monster**: Ancient magics on Skagos awaken something inside him. When severely injured or in battle rage, he transforms into a Logan Ninefingers-style berserker (the "Bloody-Nine" equivalent). He becomes seemingly unkillable, laughs maniacally, loses himself to bloodlust, fights with supernatural ferocity, and becomes a force of nature that even his own men fear. This "monster" is both a blessing (for conquest) and a curse (loss of control, moral decay, and the fear it instills in allies).
- **Skagos Adventures**: Conquering the cannibal tribes, dealing with unicorns, ancient ruins, skinchangers, and forgotten magics. Harsh survival, alliances, betrayals, and brutal warfare.
- **Return to Westeros**: After years on the island, he returns with a hardened army and claims the throne through conquest or politics, bringing the "Skagos way" to the realm.
- **Tone & Style**: Written in the style of Pierce Brown's Red Rising. Visceral, intense, poetic brutality. Heavy on internal monologue filled with rage, pain, hope, and revolutionary fire. Short, punchy sentences mixed with lyrical, metaphorical descriptions. The prose should feel raw and immediate, like Darrow's voice — personal, vengeful, and driven by a need to break chains and rise above his station. Grimdark elements from ASOIAF blended with Red Rising's revolutionary intensity.

## Success Criteria (Verifiable)
- Story covers the full arc: Arrival on Skagos → Conquering the tribes → Internal transformation (Darrow growth + monster awakening) → Return and kingship.
- Rickon's character development feels earned and shows clear progression from boy to man/monster/king, with internal voice matching Red Rising intensity.
- The "monster inside" is integrated naturally and has consequences (not just a power-up).
- Writing quality: Matches the style of Red Rising by Pierce Brown — visceral, poetic, intense prose with strong internal monologue. Better quality than initial draft. Roughly novel-length ambition (build toward substantial length across iterations, targeting 50k+ words total for a full book feel, or clear multi-chapter structure).
- No major plot holes or contradictions with core ASOIAF lore (Rickon is the youngest Stark, presumed dead, etc.).

## Constraints
- Keep it fanfiction-appropriate (dark but not gratuitous beyond the requested elements).
- Focus on Rickon as the central POV or close third-person.
- Include supporting characters (Skagosi leaders, his direwolf Shaggydog, perhaps Osha or new allies).
- The monster should feel like a curse as much as a weapon.

## Evaluation Method
- Each iteration delivers one focused section/chapter or major beat.
- Self-review + user feedback on character growth, monster integration, pacing, and quality.
- Ratchet: Only keep sections that advance the goal and meet quality bar. Revise or discard weak parts.
- Iterate until all success criteria are met and the user approves the final version.
